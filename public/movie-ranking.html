The Best Movies According to Malika

My top 3 movies of all-time.

John Wick

This movie is a great action movie. The plot is simple but effective, and the action scenes are well-choreographed and exciting. Keanu Reeves delivers a strong performance as the titular character, and the supporting cast is also solid. The cinematography and soundtrack add to the overall atmosphere of the film, making it a thrilling experience from start to finish.

The Lord of The Ring

This movie is a masterpiece of fantasy cinema. The story is epic and engaging, with well-developed characters and a richly detailed world. The special effects and cinematography are stunning, bringing the fantastical elements of the story to life in a believable way. The performances by the cast are also top-notch, with standout performances from Elijah Wood, Ian McKellen, and Viggo Mortensen. Overall, The Lord of The Rings is a must-see for any fan of fantasy or adventure films.

How to Train your Dragon

This movie is a heartwarming and visually stunning animated film. The story follows a young Viking named Hiccup who befriends a dragon, challenging the traditional beliefs of his village. The animation is beautiful, with detailed character designs and breathtaking landscapes. The voice acting is also excellent, with standout performances from Jay Baruchel and Gerard Butler. The film's themes of friendship, acceptance, and courage make it a touching and memorable experience for viewers of all ages.
