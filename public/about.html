About Me

As a developer, I enjoy creating user-friendly applications that solve real-world problems. My ability to switch between these perspectives allows me to bridge the gap between teams and ensure high-quality outcomes. I strongly believe in continuous learning, which is why I keep exploring new tools and frameworks such as React.js, MySQL, Appium, and Cypress.

Beyond work, I consider myself a motivated and collaborative individual who values teamwork, knowledge sharing, and adaptability. I am always ready to take on challenges, whether working independently or within a team environment. Outside of technology, I enjoy creative projects, engaging in meaningful discussions, and exploring ideas that inspire innovation. My ultimate goal is to grow as a professional while making a positive impact through the projects I contribute to.
